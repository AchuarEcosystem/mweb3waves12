Achuar Pre-ICO Project

For the conservation of more than 500,000 hectares of Amazon Rainforests
Proponent / Issuer: Kapawi Achuar Community of Ecuador / NASE Ecological Alliance Foundation

The Achuar - Kapawi offer you the following:

a) The creation of a foundation or direct joint association (to be constituted with the main partners and investors of the project) for the administration, management and execution of the UN REDD + Program and the conservation of its Amazonian territories; and

b) The offer of their universal Bit-CO2 tokens, which will serve to be exchanged for carbon certificates or bonds to be received by the Communities in accordance with the UN REDD + Program.

ACHUAR Pre-ICO data:

Presentation date: Nov 15, 2019.

Phases of the Project: 2 Phases.

First Phase: 31,193 Bit-CO2 tokens are offered with a maximum discount of 12% of their nominal price, amount to be collected: 274,500 euros.

Second Phase: 274,500 Bit-CO2 tokens will be offered, with a maximum discount of 10% of their nominal price, to obtain the minimum necessary initial amount of 2,470,500 euros and to attract more participants and users of the Certificates or Green Bonds.

Total number of tokens offered to Pre-ICO: 305,693 units.
Duration time: the required to obtain the minimum initial amount necessary and to attract Partners and Investors.
